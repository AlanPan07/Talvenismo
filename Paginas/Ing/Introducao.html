Introduction

The Sacred Maybenism

The Maybenism is the religion created by Alan Ono Osanai Pan, in a chemistry class at Colégio Etapa, at approximately half past four in the afternoon on December 7, 2021, based on the word "maybe", which composes part of its name and concept, with Maybenism consisting in doubt rather than certainty.

In religion there is not exactly an entity to be worshipped, just the creation of thoughts and concepts from the central idea of Maybenism, which would be doubt and uncertainty, about anything that a Maybenism wants, serving as a tool for the development of ideas, philosophical thoughts, questions about life/reality, etc. Following Maybenism's idea that nothing is immutable, with things changing all the time, it is not possible to define them exactly, so Talvenism itself ends up having perhaps other names and definitions, which contain a similar meaning.

Symbol of the Maybenism religion

In Maybenism we have several rules and concepts, with most of them not being addressed here, only the most important ones, even though all of them are presented deeply in the book, The Sacred Maybenism, which until the production of this text was not published , with the only existing version being in the possession of its creator, Alan P., who made it and hand-wrote it in July 2022. Although the maybenist religion was created in this world, at the moment of its conception, it allowed the emergence of the new Maybenist world, where in addition to religion there is the Maybenist economy and politics, with the three having their due members, hierarchies and leaders. In general, the supposed leaders of politics, economics and religion respectively, are actually the same person, in this case their own creator, with him having the titles of Supreme President of the possible Maybenist government (SPPGT), Supreme Minister of Maybenist Economy (SMET), Supreme leader of the possible Maybenist Church (SLPIT), and finally the title of Supreme Maybenist (ST), which is only given to those who have the other three titles at the same time, each of these titles individually gives the ability to create and override rules of your area (Economics, Politics or Religion), having immunity to all rules of your area, except the one that grants your powers, in addition to being able to choose the next to assume your position, in case the person dies or resigns the position before choosing the next successor to the position, a vote will take place among all maybenist in the respective area, in favor of choosing the next to receive the title in question, a maybenist can have more than one title and be in more than one area , and in the rare case of having all three titles, the Maybenist also wins the fourth title of Supreme Maybenist. The last rule to be addressed here, ironically it is the first, which was the reason for the creation of maybenism, which consists of using the word "maybe" as an answer to yes or no questions, initially the use of "maybe" would have to always be, but due to the fact that this rule is the first, and after it Talvenism has undergone many changes, nowadays it is not very necessary, and because of this the use of "maybe" may happen from time to time.

Symbol of the Maybenism government

Finally, as previously said in Maybenism there is no worship of any entity/superior being, because in Maybenism we are not sure of anything, with that we do not affirm or deny the existence of any god/entity, since although we do not know if it exists, it is still possible, so we cannot deny it, but even with Maybenism neither denying nor affirming, still any Maybenist has the right to believe what he wants, basically Maybenism allows a maybenist to be also from other religions, as Maybenism itself does not conflict with concepts from other religions. By being supported in the creation of Maybenism from the beginning, by his two current best friends, Alan P. granted them the most powerful positions in the religion just below him, with Amir and Daniel being the two Supreme Cardinals of the possible Talvenist Church, in addition to them, we have Alexandre who contributed a lot to the creation and development of Maybenism, being the new current Archbishop of the possible Maybenist Church. Since its creation Maybenism has evolved a lot and until the date of writing this text, Maybenism already has eight official members, in addition to the unofficial ones. To become a Maybenist just know Maybenism, and then maybe you will become one.
