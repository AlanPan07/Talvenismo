History

Year 2021:

  • Maybenism was created by Alan Ono Osanai Pan in 2021 in a chemistry class given by Gabriel S. at Colégio Stage.
  • Maybenism was supported by Daniel B.A., Amir H.G.K. and by Alexandre (Xandão) at its beginning in 2021 and continued to be supported gaining more followers in 2022.

Year 2022:

  • In July 2022 the book, The Sacred Maybenism, was created by Alan O.O.P., and from that he with the book was presenting the concepts of Maybenism to their classmates, thus gaining more followers.
  • Along with creating the book, Alan O.O.P. became the Leader of Maybenism.
  • On 08/03/2022, Alan got 3 new Maybenism members.

In addition, it was created economy of Maybenism (day 08/10/2022)
